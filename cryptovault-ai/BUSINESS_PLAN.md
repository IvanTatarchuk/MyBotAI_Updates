# CryptoVault AI - Business Plan & Valuation

## Executive Summary

CryptoVault AI is an enterprise-grade cryptocurrency portfolio management platform that combines advanced AI analytics with secure asset management. Our platform targets institutional investors and high-net-worth individuals seeking sophisticated tools for cryptocurrency investment.

## Market Opportunity

- **Total Addressable Market (TAM)**: $2.5 billion by 2025
- **Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)**: $500 million
- **Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)**: $50 million (10% market share)

### Target Customers

1. **Institutional Investors** (40%)

   - Family offices
   - Investment banks
   
2. **High-Net-Worth Individuals** (35%)
   - Portfolio value > $100k
   - Active traders
   
3. **Crypto Funds & DAOs** (25%)

   - Crypto venture funds
